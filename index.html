Biography

I am a Research Associate in the Biomedical Image Analysis Group, Imperial College London, working with Dr. Ben Glocker, Dr. Bernhard Kainz and Professor Daniel Rueckert.

Previously I was a postdoc at Disney Research Zurich, working on Computer Vision and Machine Learning problems related to real-time facial image capture. I earned my PhD at the University of Edinburgh in the Institute of Perception, Action and Behaviour under the supervision of Prof. Bob Fisher for my work on density estimation and large scale, multi-view 3D point set registration.

My research interests span topics in Computer Vision, Machine Learning and Medical Image Analysis. You can occasionally find me up a mountain; climbing or cycling.
